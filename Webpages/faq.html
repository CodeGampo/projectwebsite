Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

First a HZ student card is needed, once obtained it is possible to print using embedded options within the HZ Network. It is also possible to print files outside of the HZ Network, simply navigate to HZMyNetPay log in and follow the instructions.

2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Using the printers found on each floor of the HZ building it is possible to login using the HZ card. Once logged in, navigate to the scanning feature, place the document in the scanner and (unsure) select the option to send it to your school email address or a private one. Navigate to the HZ Webshop, login to the website using the given credentials. After logging in and finding the product desired click on order. Select the product and price and click Add to shoppingcart. 3. How can you book a project space?

Navigate to the HZ Self Service Portal, login using the given credentials. Once logged in click on Reservations, then Reserve a meeting or project room, read the terms and conditions and click on Reserve a room. Find a timeslot and room which suits the group, click and drag the mouse to select. Note: The rooms displayed on this page are in different building locations so make sure the correct building is chosen (GW stands for Groenewoud and is in Middelburg)! You can also add multiple rooms to the reservation by clicking the plus sign above or below the room selected. To finalize the reservation, click next on the top-right of the calendar. Write a brief description and disclose the number of participants, then click the blue Confirm reservation button to complete it.

4. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

The HZ does not offer parking spots at the campus anymore, however, there are limited spots available in the carpark on Kousteensedijk in Middelburg, the spaces are available for students, teachers and visitors. When presenting the entry card you can get an exit card at the reception desk of the JRCZ which will allow you to drive out free of charge.
